# Line: Self Is Nothing

@tag: Line_SelfIsNothing

%Short: A character expresses that they feel like they are "nothing"

## Description
A character conveys a deep sense of emptiness or insignificance, often reflecting feelings of low self-worth or existential despair. This line can be used to highlight moments of vulnerability, introspection, or emotional turmoil within a character's arc.

## Example Usage
- Nicolas: "Sometimes, I just feel like... I'm nothing. Like I don't really matter."
