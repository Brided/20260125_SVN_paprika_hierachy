# Story Beat: Internal Monologue

@tag: StoryBeat_Internal_Monologue
%Short: Extended internal reflection or self-dialogue of the PoV character

## Description
This story beat represents an extended internal monologue of the point-of-view (PoV) character. It is used to convey the character's thoughts, reflections, and self-dialogue in a more in-depth manner than a single thought line, allowing for greater exploration of their inner world.
**Input**: Character profile, internal monologue text
**Process**: Retrieve character information → Format internal monologue with character's name and text → Display monologue in the appropriate context (internal reflection)
**Output**: Formatted internal monologue displayed to the player

Uses line_thought.md for individual thoughts within the monologue.
