# Story Beat: Someone Calls Instead of Texting

@tag: StoryBeat_CallInsteadText

% Type: Event
%Short: Characters choose to call after receiving a text instead of replying via text.

## Description
This unit represents a story beat where a character opts to make a phone call in response to receiving a text message, rather than replying through text. This interaction can lead to more dynamic and personal exchanges between characters, allowing for voice tone, emotion, and immediacy to play a role in the conversation.
**Input**: text someone
**Process**: Character receives text message → Chooses to call instead of texting back → Engages in phone conversation
**Output**: Phone call dialogue, character states updated

## Notes
- Can be used independently or as part of larger story structures
- Supports branching dialogue based on the phone conversation
- Can include emotional cues conveyed through voice rather than text
