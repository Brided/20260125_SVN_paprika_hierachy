# Section: PoV By Self

%Short: The point-of-view character only thinks or speaks to themselves, there are no other character present.

## Included Ingredients
- Line: Thought
- Line: Speech (may not be used often)
- Line: Narration

- Story Beat: Internal Monologue (for extended self-reflection or internal dialogue)

## Description
This section type is used when the point-of-view (PoV) character is alone and interacts only with themselves or the player. In these sections, the narrative focuses on the internal thoughts, reflections, and self-dialogue of the PoV character without the presence of other characters.
**Input**: Character profile, internal thoughts, self-dialogue lines
**Process**: Present internal thoughts and self-dialogue in sequence → Format lines to reflect the PoV character's perspective
**Output**: Internal thoughts and self-dialogue lines displayed to the player

## Composition
- Initiate PoV Self Section → set the scene for the PoV character alone
- Internal Thoughts → present the character's thoughts and reflections
- Self-Dialogue → include any spoken lines the character says to themselves
- Conclude PoV Self Section → end the section appropriately

# Considerations
- Useful for when char acter is alone, reflecting, or making decisions
