# Concept: Theriopology

@tag: Concept_Theriopology

%Short: Study of animal behavior and social structures, anthropology of this world.

- "Humanity" in this world is "Personhood"
- "Anthropology" in this world is "Theriopology"

## Definition

Theriopology: an academic discipline focused on population behaviour, social structures, and cultural practices of anthropomorphic animals in this world.

Personhood: term encompassing all the population of anthropomorphic species in this world
